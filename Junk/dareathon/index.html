David's Dareathon

Recently, I have had a lot of new projects I've wanted to start but I've had a hard time getting myself motivated to take the plunge on any of them. What I'm going to do is force myself to publicly marathon my way through the first day of each of them. I will give myself 24 straight hours to get a rough version of each project finished. I will aim to do one of these each month. I plan to livetweet, livestream, liveblog, and livechat as much as possible during each. At the very least, I will motivate myself to learn new stuff and make you laugh at my ineptitude and shame. At the very best, maybe I'll inspire you to dareathon your own dream projects. If so, I'd love to hear from you!
